Health-Exercise

I am writing this as a means to push it deep into my brains that this is the bare necessity I need to follow everyday.

Daily Bath

Taking a bath daily is a necessity. It helps get rids of the odour causing particles in your body, and cleanse you before you can apply fragrance. It is adviced to use a less fragrant soap and shampoo for bathing so as to not interfere its smell with the perfume you plan to apply.

It is also of utmost importance to dry yourself properly after a bath, to get rid of odour creating bacteria

Moisturizing

Make sure to moisturize yourself properly after your shower, as a dehydrated body, tends to create more odour. Moisturizing also helps to retain the smell of the fragrance for much longer

Deodarant

If it is a normal day, make sure to spray yourself with antipresperant and deodarant right after your bath to stop your body from creating unnecessary odour

Perfume

On important events/days one must make use of a perfume. The proper etiquette of applying a perfume is 6 sprays, on your wrists each, behind your ears and on your neck left and right

Hair

Make sure to properly wash your hair everyday, in my opinion its better to apply shampoo everyday to remove the odour from your hair, and keep it less oily

Drink Water

Last but not least I have learnt that it is always good to keep your body hydrated!
